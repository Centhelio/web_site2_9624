OUR EXPERIENCE

Our team is composed of students with different competencies. Here is a overview of two of our past projects:

Surgeon robot

In collaboration with École Supérieur de Technologies (ETS), we were tasked with building a robot with a movable arm capable of performing heart surgeries. This project allowed us to gain experience in project management, engineering, constructing electrical circuits, programming and mechanical work.

Pop bridge

For this project, we were asked to build a Pop Bridge using limited materials such as popsicles and white glue. The bridge had to be able to withstand as much weight as posible. Planning, designing and creativity were some of the competencies we developed during this project.
