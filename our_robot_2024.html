Our robot's strengths

Precision

We strive to build precision in every component of our Robot. This increases our Robot’s shooting capabilities, enables more fluid movements and allows more precise maneuvers.

Robustness

We invested time, thought and multiple iterations to ensure our Robot is robust. We developed a sturdy design and used aluminium material to ensure Spark is solid, agile, and reliable.

Unique Design

Our robot’s unique design is a result of teamwork, creativity, and rigorous work processes. We used CADs, built prototypes, and leveraged A&I to test, learn and refine our design.
